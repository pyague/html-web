Mujeres al borde de un ataque de nervios

Directed by Pedro Almodovar

Pepa and Iván are dubbing actors. He is an inveterate womanizer and, after a long relationship, he breaks up
with Pepa: he leaves her a message on the answering machine asking her to pack a suitcase with his things.
Pepa, who can't stand living in a house full of memories, decides to rent it out.
While waiting for Iván to pick up his suitcase, his house fills up with extravagant people from whom
he will learn many things about loneliness and madness.

BCarmen Maura and Antonio Banderas
