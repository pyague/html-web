Jeux d'enfants

Directed by Yann Samuell

As adults, best friends Julien and Sophie continue the odd game they started as children
-- a fearless competition to outdo one another with daring and outrageous stunts.
While they often act out to relieve one another's pain, their game might be a way to avoid the fact
that they are truly meant for one another.

Guillaume Canet and Marion Cotillard
