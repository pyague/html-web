Lord of the Rings

Directed by Peter Jackson

The Lord of the Rings, a shy young hobbit named Frodo Baggins inherits a simple gold ring.
He knows the ring has power, but not that he alone holds the secret to the survival--or enslavement--of the
entire world. Now Frodo, accompanied by a wizard, an elf, a dwarf, two men and three loyal hobbit friends,
must become the greatest hero the world has ever known to save the land and the people he loves.
