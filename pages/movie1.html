Seven

Directed by David Fincher

Somerset is a veteran detective who is about to retire and who meets the young policeman Mills.
Together they must investigate the murder of an obese man who has been tied up and forced to eat to death.
The crimes take place while the two detectives show their opposite moods, one patient and rational, and the
more impetuous young man.

Brad Pitt and Morgan Freeman
